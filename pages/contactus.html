Contacting the Data Science Club

Written on February 24th, 2019 by Eric Englin

As a student organization at the Harvard Kennedy School, the Data Science Club is always looking for new opportunities and projects.

To work with us, please email: Eric Englin (eric_englin@student.hks.harvard.edu) or Jake Schneider (jake_schneider@student.hks.harvard.edu)

Other ways to contact us are:

  • Google Form: Completing this form will get you onto our email list
  • Slack: This will have same event information and more data science discussion
  • Github: This will have our latest projects and code for this website as well. Feel free to use anything that you'd like!
